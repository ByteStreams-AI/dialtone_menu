Will my customers know how it works?
Customers call your same number, place their order, and get updates by text. They don’t need an app, an account, or anything new.
What if a customer asks something complicated?
DialTone.Menu uses your live menu for pricing, specials, availability, modifiers, substitutions, and item details. If something requires staff input, it can transfer or take a message.
Do I need new hardware?
No. If you already have a computer, tablet, or phone, DialTone.Menu works with your existing hardware.
How long does setup take?
DialTone.Menu works with your existing phone number. To use your existing number, setup can take 1 - 5 days, depending on your provider. For new numbers, less than a day.
