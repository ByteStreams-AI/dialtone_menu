On the kitchen display, before the customer hangs up

The order shows up where the food gets made.

The moment the customer confirms, the order is on your kitchen display. No paper ticket to lose. No staff member shouting modifiers across the line. Each card shows items, modifiers, allergen flags, and an order number your team can actually call out.

Modifiers built in

Mild, medium, hot. Sauce on the side. No cilantro. Structured choices, not buried notes.

Allergens flagged

Severe allergies get a red banner at the top of the card before prep begins.

Real order numbers

"Order forty-seven" works on the phone, receipt, and counter handoff.

Within 30 seconds of confirming

They tap. You're paid.

Right after the call, the customer gets a text with their order details and a payment link. They tap, confirm the receipt, add a tip if they want, and pay before pickup. That means no declined cards at the counter and no chasing down walkaways.

Apple Pay · Google Pay · Link · Visa · Mastercard · Amex
Tips, not arm-twisting. The tip prompt appears on checkout, not in the voice flow. Customers choose preset or custom amounts, and tips go directly to your account.
