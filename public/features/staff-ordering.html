Staff Ordering

One screen. Anyone can run a table.

DialTone's staff ordering system turns a phone or tablet into a full table-service station. Seat a party, open a check, take rounds all night, fire to the kitchen, and settle up — with a layout so simple a brand-new hire is useful on their first shift. No thousand-dollar terminal, no week of training.

🧾 Staff ordering — coming soon

The whole floor, at a glance.

A live tables board shows what's open, what's reserved, who's seated, and which tables need bussing. Tap a table to seat a party and open their check — walk-in or reservation, it's one tap.

  • Open · Reserved · Seated · Needs-bussing, in real time
  • Upcoming reservations shown right on the table
  • Tap a reservation to seat it — the check opens pre-filled
🍽️Tables board — coming soon

Take rounds all night on one check.

Unlike a pay-first counter order, a table's check stays open and grows over the meal. Add a round, add another, apply a discount — the check re-prices itself every time, and regulars are recognized so their favorites and allergies come right up.

  • Open checks that accumulate rounds across the meal
  • One-tap add with a persistent cart fly-out
  • Regulars recognized by phone — favorites & allergies surfaced
➕Open check — coming soon

Fire to the kitchen. Watch it come up.

Each round fires straight to the Kitchen and Bar displays. When the food or drinks are ready, the table lights up on the server's screen with a chime — so they run it hot instead of checking the pass.

  • Rounds fire to the right station automatically
  • Food-ready and drinks-ready signals back to the server
  • An audible alert the moment a table's order is up
🔥Fire & ready signals — coming soon

Settle up, turn the table.

Close the check with cash or card — including Tap to Pay — add a tip, and clear the table for the next party in a couple of taps. The reservation completes, the table frees, and the floor's ready for whoever's next.

  • Cash, card, and Tap to Pay settlement with tip presets
  • Close & turn — the table frees for the next party
  • Every settle reconciles in the admin dashboard
💵Settle & turn — coming soon
