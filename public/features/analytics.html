Features / Analytics
Analytics

Run the restaurant on numbers, not hunches.

DialTone turns every call, order, and payment into plain-English numbers — sales over time, your busiest days and hours, and how many first-timers come back. No spreadsheets to build, no POS export to wrangle; it's all in your dashboard, updating as the orders come in.

Because DialTone takes the order, it already knows what sold, when, and to whom — so the reporting is a byproduct of running service, not a separate chore at the end of the night.

Sales and orders over time.

Track revenue and order volume across any date range, with the average ticket right beside them. See whether this week beat last week at a glance, instead of adding up receipts.

  • Revenue and order counts over any date range
  • Average ticket size and how it's trending
  • Paid, cash, and channel breakdowns in one place

Your busiest days and hours.

Every chart is labeled by day of the week, so the Friday rush and the slow Tuesday are obvious. Staff to the demand you actually have instead of guessing at the schedule.

  • Orders and revenue broken out by day of week
  • Peak dayparts, so you schedule to real demand
  • Spot the quiet windows worth a promotion

New faces and regulars.

See how many customers are new versus returning, how your loyalty tiers are filling out, and whether the people you serve are coming back. The repeat-visit engine, measured.

  • New vs. returning customers over time
  • Loyalty tier distribution and point activity
  • Repeat-visit trends that show retention working

Kitchen performance, in real time.

Track how the kitchen is actually moving: average and median cook time, fire-to-table timing, pass wait, and station-level load across kitchen and bar. These live metrics help leads catch slowdowns early and tighten execution before tickets stack up.

  • Cook-time trends by day, station, and service window
  • Fire-to-table and pass timing visibility
  • Built into the admin dashboard — nothing extra to open
  • Coming soon: menu-item cook-time analytics

Stop guessing. Start knowing.
