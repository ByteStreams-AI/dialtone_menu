Dynamic Menu

Edit once. Live everywhere.

DialTone's dynamic restaurant menu is the single source of truth for every channel. Change a price, 86 an item, add a modifier, or update your hours — and it's live on the phone agent, the app, the kiosk, and the counter in seconds. No reprinting, no re-keying, no stale specials.

Change anything in seconds.

Ran out of the special? Two taps and it's gone from every screen and the phone agent's mouth. Bump a price for the weekend, then bump it back Monday. The menu bends to your day instead of the other way around.

  • Toggle availability instantly — items 86'd everywhere at once
  • Prices in exact cents, no rounding, no drift
  • Today's specials surface automatically

Modifiers that actually behave.

Build option groups with required and optional choices, minimums and maximums, and per-option price changes. Every channel — voice, app, kiosk — enforces the same rules, so the order is always priced right and always makeable.

  • Required / optional groups with min–max limits
  • Per-option price deltas, snapshotted onto each order
  • Alcohol and special-instruction handling built in

One menu behind every channel.

The phone agent, your app, the self-service kiosk, and your staff's ordering screen all read the same live menu. Update it in one place and there's simply no way for them to disagree — no reconciling four systems at close.

  • Serving windows — brunch shows at brunch, dinner at dinner
  • Spoken pronunciation overrides so the agent nails every dish
  • Operating hours drive when each channel takes orders
