DialTone POS (Staff)

Your whole POS. In a server's pocket.

DialTone POS is a mobile point of sale that runs on the phone already in your server's apron. Fire tableside orders, take the payment with a tap, and print a receipt on the spot — a full restaurant POS with no thousand-dollar terminal to buy or bolt to the counter.

It's a native app for iPhone and Android that runs alongside the system you have today, or replaces it. Every sale flows into the same orders, kitchen, and reporting as the rest of DialTone, so the floor and the back office never fall out of sync.

Check splitting has never been easier.

Split the check any way the table wants — by party size, by seat, or any combination in between. Every guest pays their own way - tap to pay or cash, with their own receipt, and the whole thing settles in seconds without a trip back to the terminal.

  • Split check by party size
  • Split check by seat
  • Any combination of payments
  • Tap to pay at table
  • Individual printed receipts

Loyalty context appears the moment they're seated.

When a returning loyalty guest is seated, servers immediately see the profile details that matter in service: allergy flags, recent orders, and aggregate ordering trends. That context helps staff guide the table faster, avoid repeat mistakes, and personalize recommendations without leaving the POS flow.

  • Allergy and dietary alerts shown at table open
  • Last orders visible for quick repeat suggestions
  • Aggregate order data highlights guest preferences over time

One check, open all night — then hand it off.

A table's check stays open and grows over the meal, firing each round to the Kitchen and Bar displays. Servers can hand a table off mid-shift, and every settle reconciles in the admin dashboard.

  • Open checks that accumulate rounds across the meal
  • Hand a table off between servers without losing the check
  • Every sale reconciles in the admin dashboard
