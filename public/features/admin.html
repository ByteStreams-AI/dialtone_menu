Admin

Every call, every order — in one place.

The DialTone restaurant admin dashboard is the back office for the whole operation: live orders, call transcripts, reservations, and payments in real time — and the settings that drive it all, from your menu and hours to staff, branding, and analytics. One login instead of five.

Live orders, with the whole story.

Watch orders arrive in real time and open any one for the full picture — items, totals, payment status, and a timeline of exactly when it was placed, paid, prepped, and picked up. For dine-in, a live kitchen signal shows whether the food's up.

  • Real-time order feed with status filters
  • Per-order lifecycle timeline — placed → paid → ready → done
  • Refunds, resends, and cancellations handled in place
🧾Order detail — coming soon

Calls and reservations, on the record.

Every call the voice agent handles is logged with its outcome; every reservation lands on a timeline and a floor plan you can actually see. Assign tables, track no-shows, and manage the book without a paper ledger.

  • Call history with outcomes and transcript references
  • Reservations timeline + spatial floor plan
  • Reminder texts fire automatically
🗓️Reservations & floor — coming soon

Know your numbers.

Sales and orders by day, loyalty and customer trends, day-of-week patterns — the analytics that tell you what's actually working, without exporting anything to a spreadsheet.

  • Sales & order analytics, day-of-week labeled
  • Loyalty and customer insights
  • Transaction history and CSV export

Set it up your way.

Menu, modifiers, hours, tax, delivery, branding, staff, payments, and your voice-agent greeting — all self-serve. Change what you need, when you need it, and it's live everywhere in seconds.

  • Menu, hours, tax, and delivery settings
