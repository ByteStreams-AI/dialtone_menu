Features / Customer App
Customer App

Your restaurant. In their pocket.

DialTone gives you a branded restaurant ordering app under your own name and colors, where your regulars browse the menu, order pickup or delivery, pay in a tap, book a table, and earn loyalty points. Your customers, your brand, your data — not a third-party marketplace's.

The app wears your restaurant's name, logo, and colors, one white-label build themed for you at runtime. Customers download your app, not a generic one buried under a hundred other restaurants.

Order, pay, done.

Browse the live menu, customize with modifiers, and check out with a native, secure card payment — for pickup on your schedule or DoorDash delivery to the door. The order is priced by your system and drops straight into the kitchen.

  • Pickup with scheduled times, or DoorDash Drive delivery
  • Native in-app card payment — paid to you directly
  • Every order re-priced server-side; the app never guesses

Points that bring them back.

Built-in loyalty rewards every order automatically. Customers see their balance, tier, and progress, then redeem points right at checkout — a repeat-visit engine that lives inside your own app instead of a punch card that gets lost.

  • Earn on every paid order; tiers with multipliers
  • Redeem points at checkout for an instant discount
  • A full ledger of activity, always in sync

Reservations and reminders, built in.

Guests book a table from the app in a few taps — checked against your real availability — and get a reminder before they arrive. Push notifications keep them posted when the order's confirmed and ready.

  • In-app booking against live table availability
  • Automatic reservation reminder texts
  • Order-status push notifications
