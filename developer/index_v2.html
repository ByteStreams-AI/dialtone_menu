How It Works

Live in 48 hours. Four simple steps.

We do the heavy lifting. You approve. It goes live. That's it.

01
Discovery

We learn your restaurant inside and out

On a single 30-minute onboarding call, we capture everything your AI needs to know — your hours, your menu, your reservation policy, your most-asked questions, and the tone you want guests to experience. You talk. We listen. We build.

02
Build

We configure and voice your AI host

Our team programs your DialTone AI with your specific responses, your preferred caller flow, and a voice that fits your brand — warm and casual for a neighborhood bistro, polished and precise for a fine-dining room. We handle every detail before you hear a word of it.

Who It's Not For

We'd rather say it plainly than waste your time.

DialTone.Menu isn't for everyone. If any of these sound like you, we're probably not the right fit — and that's okay.

  • ✕ You want complete DIY control — logging into dashboards, editing scripts yourself, and tweaking responses on the fly
  • ✕ You run a fast-food or counter-service model where phone orders and reservations aren't part of your operation
  • ✕ You're part of a corporate chain with an existing IT infrastructure, centralized phone systems, or a dedicated call center
  • ✕ You're looking for an online ordering integration or a full POS replacement — this handles the phone, not the ticket printer
  • ✕ You want zero-cost or completely free — we charge a fair monthly rate for a professionally managed service
