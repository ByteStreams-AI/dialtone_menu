# Pilot Milestones

With a 60-day pilot, your internal milestone cadence needs to be tighter. I'd suggest:

## Milestones

- Day 1–7: Onboarding call + menu setup + first live calls
- Day 14: Check-in on call volume and any issues
- Day 30: Mid-pilot review — are they using it? Getting value? Give feedback?
- Day 45: Conversion conversation — "Here's what we'd recommend for you: Starter or Pro"
- Day 55: Contract decision deadline, Stripe subscription set up
- Day 60: Clean handoff to paid tier or wind-down
